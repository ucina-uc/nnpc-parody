A Global
Employment Of Choice

Building on our track record as one of the best organizations to work in Nigeria, NNPC Limited aspires to remain an employer of choice for great talent who are passionate about solving problems on a global scale.

At NNPC Limited, we provide meaningful work, an inclusive and safe working environment, exciting career opportunities and continuous growth for our people.

Diversity & Inclusion

At NNPC Limited, our diverse experiences and perspectives are forged into a shared commitment to excellence by our inclusive culture and practices.

Respect for the individual is at the heart of our work culture

We provide a workplace where the best talents across nationalities, ethnicities, gender, and generations can thrive and contribute to providing the energy the world needs.

A Place for Growth

NNPC Ltd’s business strategy is focused on aggressive growth across the energy value chain and rapid expansion of our footprints globally. We actively seek and develop great people, unleashing their highest potential through extensive talent development, coaching, mentoring and career development programmes. This is reflected in our Human Capital Management priorities revolving around:

Growing our technical, professional and leadership capacity


Creating opportunities for our top performers to grow and thrive


Proactively planning succession & providing strong leadership development opportunities

Work with NNPC Ltd

Do you desire to work with a team of people passionate about powering economies, enterprises, and people across the globe?

Are you energized enough to work with and learn from some of the most talented and experienced professionals in various fields?

We provide a range of opportunities for graduates, experienced professionals and interns to contribute their unique ideas to solving global energy challenges.

Internship Opportunities

Our commitment to sustainable growth in partnership with our communities drives our investment in the local talent pipeline through internship programmes. These programmes offer real life business and professional experience which provide a foundation for fruitful future careers.
We currently offer internship opportunities only in Nigeria in line with applicable
local laws.

Graduate Opportunities

Our flagship Young Professionals Development (YPD) Programme provides an exciting career start and a clear path to professional and business leadership for graduates. The 3-year programme combines foundational technical and business training, early career coaching and mentoring, immersive competency-based development opportunities across various roles to prepare the right
quality of future leaders for our business.

Experienced Professionals

As an experienced professional, NNPC Limited offers a new vista to deploy your experience and leadership to drive growth and business excellence in a global brand. You will be joining a team of professionals, passionate about collaborating to solve humanity’s energy challenges.
